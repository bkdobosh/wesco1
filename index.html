The Vision

From humble beginnings to a hockey mecca in Southern Connecticut. With the support of die-hard fans like you, Wesco has grown to be a trusted and reliable source for all of your ice hockey needs - for close to forty years and counting. Our commitment to growing and serving you is evident in our vast selection of gear and a 3 room facility dedicated specifically to goaltenders. Our knowledgable sales staff will provide you with a level of service and information that you cannot find anywhere else. We look forward to serving you both online, as well as in person in our Brookfield, Connecticut store. And like Denis Leary says, "If we don't have it, it doesn't exist!"

-Doug Mathews and the Wesco Team

Goalie Equipment

Over the past 40+ years we have built our Goalie Center to 3 full rooms. At WESCO professional coaches and players give expert advice and training techniques for all ages and levels of play. All of our staff is educated and well trained to properly fit and sell the newest goalie equipment from all the top equipment manufacturers including: Bauer®, Brians®, Brown®, CCM®, Eddy®, Graf®, Grit®, Reebok®, Sherwood®, Tackla®, TPS®, Vaughn®, Wall®, and Warrior®. Along with all the newest and best goalie equipment on the market, we have our own state-of-the-art leg pad demo area, and a full training station to give all our goalies an edge over their competition. At WESCO we know goalies. We cater to the every need of every goalie, every day. We pride ourselves on providing the best customer service and expert knowledge of anyone in the area. Come on in and experience the WESCO Goalie Center, you see why people are saying, "You have never seen a store for goalie equipment unless you've been to WESCO!"
